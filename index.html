This page demonstrates how to use JavaScript to change the image, color and message of the page depending on the time of day. The page will change according to the time of the day as they will change if the day is either Morning, Afternoon, Evening or night.

There is also the time and day of the week listed on the page as it will change to show the current time in the page and change the to the correct day of the week. These are accomplised by using If and else statements.

The goal of the project was to change aspects of the page depending on the time. With Morning (5am to 12 noon) the image would change to associated with Morning and the color would orange and the message "Good Moring!"

Afternoon (12 noon to 6pm) the image would change to associated with Afternoon and the color would green and the message "Good Afternoon!"

Evening (6pm to 11pm) the image would change to associated with Evening and the color would red and the message "Good Evening!"!

Night (11pm to 5am) the image would change to associated with Night and the color would blue and the message "Good Night!"
